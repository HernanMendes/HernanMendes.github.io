Information About me

I am a passionate software developer with an electronic engineering background. My interest in programming, robotics, and IoT technology sparked during my electronic technician studies and led me to pursue a degree in electronic engineering.

At university, I discovered my true passion for programming, so I became a teaching assistant for the C language fundamentals course and a research intern in the development of applications for embedded systems. After completing four years of my degree, in 2021, I shifted my focus entirely to software development and start working as a Python developer at BB-media and then at Gregario, performing as a web scraper.

Moving forward, I aspire to expand my knowledge as a backend developer and work on meaningful projects that use data to make a positive social impact, helping to create a better world through technology.
